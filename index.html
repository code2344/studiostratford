How we work

1. Scope

Mapping your aspirations and ways of working in a focused discovery conversation.

2. Connect

Collaborating in regular conversations in person or online to turn those aspirations into meaningful, practical actions.

3. Refine

Assessing actions and refining them to advance your aspirations further.

4. Close

Reflecting on the journey and its possibilities for long term change in your life.

About Elaine

Elaine is a deeply experienced researcher, writer, editor, and coach with decades of professional practice and mentoring. Her work helps clients become incisively expressive, purposeful, and aligned across thinking, practice, and priorities.

Qualifications: BA (Honours IA) (Flinders), Masters in Tertiary Education Management (Melbourne), PhD (Adelaide), and Certificate IV in Life Coach Practice (LCA). She is also a member of the International Coaching Federation.
